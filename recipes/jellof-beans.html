Jellof-Beans Recipe

Description

Jellof Beans holds a dear place in my heart and I think this meal is sweeter than love hehehehe. There are varieties of beans you can use to make this meal, and different methods preparation but in this tiny article, I will be giving you the ingredients and the method I use to make my own sweet jellof beans.

I guarantee you that after studying this article, you will be able to make your own sweet beans.

Ingredients

  • Nigerian Brown Beans
  • 1 bulb of onion
  • 10 Fresh red pepper
  • Half cup of palm oil
  • 2 Maggi cube
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Boil water
  2. Rinse beans and pour into the boiling water
  3. After about 5 minutes, rinse beans to remove excess protein
  4. Boil another water
  5. Pour in the rinsed beans into the boiling water and check continously until the beans is soft
  6. Chop your bulb of onion and grind the fresh red pepper
  7. When the beans becomes soft, pour in all your ingredients one by one. The last thing to put in should be the palm oil
  8. Check continously until it becomes thick
  9. Serve your sweet jellof beans
